01 — Professional Summary

Where operations meets engineering

I am a Customer Operations Professional, Accounts Receivable Specialist, and AI-Assisted Business Systems Developer with 9+ years of experience spanning retail sales, customer service, administrative management, and financial operations.

My journey began on the retail floor at Zara and H&M, advancing to cashier and assistant store manager roles at Aeropostale. I then transitioned into corporate operations at eNovate as Assistant Manager of Administrative Affairs, before finding my niche as a Senior Collection Specialist, handling accounts receivable, debt recovery, and billing operations.

Combining my deep operational expertise with self-taught technology skills, I founded Axentro — a startup focused on building AI-powered business systems. I leverage AI-assisted development tools, GitHub, Supabase, and Google Apps Script to design, deploy, and maintain real-world business systems that solve operational friction.
